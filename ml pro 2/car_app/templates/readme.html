DIRECTIONS FOR THE USE OF APP

STEP 1:- Click on our website


STEP 2:- You will land on Login Page where you have to enter your username and password if you are already registered or else click on New Users and get yourself Register


STEP 3:- On Visiting register page you enter your username and set password and again enter your password to confirm it


STEP 4:- Now when you are loggedIn you will land on home page


STEP 5:- Enter Details of car



NOTE DOWN
1> Total Kilometers driven range:- 1-806599 (Enter km driven)
2> Purchasing Year of Car range:- 1992-2020 (Enter purchasing year of car)
3> Select Car Brand
4> Select the transmission of car
5> Select the seller type of car
6> Select the fuel type of car
7> Select the ownership details of car

STEP 6:- Now once you click on Show Price button you will get the selling price of car
